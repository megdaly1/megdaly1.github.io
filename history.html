History

Cleves and Loneman Jewelers has been open since 1932 and was opened by Ed Cleves Sr. and Joe Lonnneman. They opened the store after being fired from Motch Jewelers during the Depression. When the two men retired it was then passed down to Ed Cleves Jr. and his wife Gertrude. Lonneman was no longer apart of the buisness but they kept the name to honnor the hardwork of both men. Charlie and Mary Lynn took over ownership of the store in 1989 upon Nick's retirement. Gert continued working at the store until 1997. He began working at the store full time in 2004. He and Erin were married in 2005. They then took ownership of the store January 1st 2017. Charlie and Mary Lynn are easing their way into retirement and are still working at the store part time.
