History

Cleves and Loneman Jewelers has been open since 1932 and was opened by Ed Cleves Sr. and Joe Lonnneman. They opened the store after being fired from Motch Jewelers during the Depression. When the two men retired it was then passed down to Ed Cleves Jr. and his wife Gertrude. Lonneman was no longer apart of the buisness but they kept the name to honnor the hardwork of both men.
